Sita Ramam is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language period romantic drama film written and directed by Hanu Raghavapudi.[4] Produced by Vyjayanthi Movies and Swapna Cinema, the film stars Mrunal Thakur and Dulquer Salmaan in the titular role, with Rashmika Mandanna, Sumanth, Sachin Khedekar and others in the supporting role. The movie marks Thakur's debut in Tollywood. Set in 1964, Lieutenant Ram, an orphaned army officer serving at the Kashmir border, gets anonymous love letters from Sita Mahalakshmi, after which Ram is on a mission to find Sita and propose his love.
